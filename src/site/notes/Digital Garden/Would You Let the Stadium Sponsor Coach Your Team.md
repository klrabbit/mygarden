When I read or even see articles about science or health committees that the Trump Administration has taken over, they are almost always filled with people who have no expertise in the subject the committees are about.  The new [President’s Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST),] (https://www.science.org/content/article/trump-s-new-science-panel-stuffed-high-tech-billionaires) has just one scientist on it. I start thinking, what if an NFL pro team developed their coaching staff the way the Trump administration does their committees? First, they would fire all the coaches, not because of the team record but because they didn't like the way they coached. Then they would hire the guy the stadium is named after to be the head coach and fill the coaching roster with people who run sports stores, brands, and organizations. None who have any coaching experience. Nobody in their right mind would think this was a good idea, but we accept this as normal for the Trump Administration, especially when it involves science.
